Frequently Asked Questions

When should I apply for passport?

The passport can be applied for reissue from one year before its expiry or for change in the entries in the existing passport including complete name change, change of name after marriage, change in appearance, change in date of birth, change in place of birth, change in sex or change in signature.

Is GST applicable to all businesses?

GST is applicable to businesses with an annual turnover exceeding the prescribed threshold limit. GST rates vary depending on the type of goods or services. There are multiple slabs, including 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%.

What is an International Driving Permit (IDP)?

An International Driving Permit is an official translation of a person's home country driving license into multiple languages. It is often required or recommended for driving in foreign countries.

Information any Passport:

Regular Indian nationals who want to travel abroad for either business or leisure are issued a common passport, which is also referred to as passport type P. These passports are navy blue in colour and are issued for business trips, vacations, and educational trips. Therefore, the vast majority of Indians hold this passport.

Passport Pcc

Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) is issued to Indian Passport holders in case they have applied for Residential Status, Employment or Long term visa or for immigration. PCC cannot be issued for persons going abroad on Tourist Visa.

Benefits of GST In India

The GST framework consolidates various indirect taxes such as Service Tax, Excise Duty, and Value Added Tax, promoting simplicity and reducing the tax burden. As a significant reform, GST holds numerous advantages for both businesses and the general public in India.
